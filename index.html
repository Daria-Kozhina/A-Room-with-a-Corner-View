A Room with A Corner View is an investigation into the meaning of a corner beyond its archetypical shape in the context of punishment and guilt. It is an anatomy of the unknown within the intermediary realm of grim charm where reality meets the conceiving rumbles of entities.

Please utilise the full-screen function. Headphones or high-end speakers are recommended

In depth concept explanatory video

The animation serves as a product of character studies that translate four stages of punishment: resistance, confinement, contemplation and confession.

Each of four stages will be physically presented along with the animation during the Graduation Festival 2020 in KABK, The Hague, The Netherlands.
